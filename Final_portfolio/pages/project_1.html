ACE

I am the graphic design intern for the Alliance for Catholic Education (ACE) based on Notre Dame’s campus. I work in the office daily making various promotional materials for the organization. The tasks vary greatly depending on the day. I could be making posters, pop-up banners, email graphics, annual reports, or even work on the upcoming Christmas card. It has been a wonderful experience that has taught me to be flexible, work with tight deadlines, and enter the back-and-forth dialogue necessary when working with a client, so that the best final product that they want can be produced.
Pop-Up Banners
This was a series of pop-up banners I created for ACE to help their recruiting events. They wanted a poster that would connect well with the vision of Catholic education from Fr. Theodore Hesburgh, a former president of the University of Notre Dame.
Below are a variety of banner options I made before the client narrowed down the direction he wanted and we finally settled on the final poster on the right.
