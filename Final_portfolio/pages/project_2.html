Classy Animals

This was a series I created to make fun and refined portrait posters of animals. the goal is always to have the upmost class.
Process
Each of the animals was created through digital painting in Adobe Photoshop. After doing a fair bit of visual research on the animal, I work out a sketch, then complete the painting in black and white. Once the black and white image is to my liking, I work in the color, until I arrive at my finished product.
“A gentleman is one who never hurts anyone’s feelings unintentionally.”

Products
I then took the series of images to create products that would be fun to own. The first iteration was a poster, then I also created the design for a trendy T-Shirt.
